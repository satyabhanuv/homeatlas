What's covered & how it works
Type a service (farmers market, free clinic, school...) in the first box, then tap Search. Leave the location box empty (or type "Near me") and your browser will share your location when you submit. You can also type a city, ZIP, or full address there. Suggestions appear after 3 characters. US-only for now. Privacy & data

We do not store your address on Nearnity servers. To resolve a location and load nearby results, your browser may send the address or coordinates to public map and geocoding services (OpenStreetMap-based providers, US Census Geocoder, federal data APIs like NWS / USGS / NPS). These are public-data services with their own privacy policies linked below.

Saved places stay on your device. Anything you star is stored in your browser's local storage only — not on Nearnity's servers, not synced. Clearing your browser data removes them.

Email digest is opt-in. If (and only if) you subscribe to weekly updates, we store your email + saved-place location in our database to send those emails. Unsubscribe anytime. No third-party sharing, ever.
