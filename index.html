Privacy & data

We don’t store your address. Search runs entirely in your browser. The address text is sent to OpenStreetMap-based services to convert it to coordinates and look up nearby schools — nothing is saved on our side.

When we add user accounts, saving an address will be opt-in and used to power personalized recommendations.
